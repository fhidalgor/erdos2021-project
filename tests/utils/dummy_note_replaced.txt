Different electrocardiographic changes have been described during
thrombolytic therapy for acute myocardial infarction to indicate successful reperfusion the occluded
coronary artery also can be reopened by percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty ptca this study was
performed to compare electrocardiographic changes during primary or rescue ptca
and thrombolytic therapy the electrocardiographic changes were studied directly
at the moment of reperfusion during ptca.
